Frequently Asked Questions

What is CardWise used for?

CardWise is a flashcard study platform designed specifically for IB students. It helps you ace your exams by providing curated flashcards and chapter quizzes for core subjects like Economics, Physics, Business Management, Computer Science, and Spanish. Our spaced-repetition system makes recall effortless and study time efficient.

What subjects does CardWise cover?

Currently, CardWise offers comprehensive study materials for Economics, Physics, Business Management, Computer Science, and Spanish. Each subject is organized by units and chapters, with more subjects coming soon!

Do I need to create an account?

Yes, creating an account allows you to track your progress, save your quiz attempts, and access all features of CardWise. Sign up is quick and easy using your email address.

Can I use CardWise on mobile?

Yes! CardWise is fully responsive and works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. Study anywhere, anytime, on any device.

Is CardWise really free to use?

Yes, CardWise is completely free for IB students. We believe quality education resources should be accessible to everyone.
